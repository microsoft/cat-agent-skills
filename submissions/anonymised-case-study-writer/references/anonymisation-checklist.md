# Anonymisation Checklist

Use this checklist before returning any case study derived from client, project or engagement material.

## Remove or generalise

- Client or customer names.
- Programme names that identify the organisation.
- Individual names, job titles or team names unless public and approved.
- Exact locations where they could identify the client.
- Exact dates unless publicly disclosed or approved.
- Internal system names, architecture details or commercial data.
- Contract values, confidential metrics or sensitive operating details.
- Screenshots, extracts or quotes that contain identifying information.

## Safer descriptors

Use descriptors such as:

- A global consumer goods company.
- A large public sector organisation.
- A regulated financial services organisation.
- A national healthcare provider.
- A multinational energy business.
- A complex enterprise technology function.

## Evidence safeguards

- If a metric is not provided, do not invent one.
- If a result is implied but not evidenced, describe it as a capability shift or observed outcome only if source text supports that wording.
- If the client is recognisable from the combination of clues, generalise further.
- When in doubt, include an evidence gap rather than filling the gap.
