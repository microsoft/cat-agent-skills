# CFP Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to review a conference session abstract before returning it.

## Title

- Specific enough to stand out.
- Not just a broad topic label.
- Clear about the tension, outcome or audience value.
- Avoids hype unless appropriate for the event.

## Abstract

- Explains why the topic matters now.
- States who the session is for.
- Makes a clear promise about what attendees will learn.
- Avoids invented credentials, claims or event-specific details.
- Fits the requested word or character limit when supplied.

## Takeaways

- Practical and distinct from each other.
- Written from the attendee's perspective.
- Suitable for the stated audience level.
- Avoid vague outcomes such as "understand the importance of" unless expanded.

## Speaker information

- Uses only supplied or public speaker information.
- Does not invent awards, roles, clients or experience.
- Keeps the bio proportionate to the event format.
