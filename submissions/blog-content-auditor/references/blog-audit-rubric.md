# Blog Audit Rubric

Score from 0 to 5 against each dimension.

## 1. Reader promise
Is it clear why the reader should care and what they will gain?

## 2. Opening strength
Does the opening create relevance quickly without relying on generic hooks?

## 3. Argument shape
Does the post build a coherent point of view rather than list observations?

## 4. Evidence and examples
Are claims supported by examples, experience, data, references or practical detail?

## 5. Structure and scanability
Is the post easy to scan, navigate and understand?

## 6. Distinctiveness
Does the post say something specific, or could it belong to anyone?

## 7. Next action
Does the post help the reader think, decide or act differently?

## Editorial action labels

- Keep: strong and current.
- Refresh: mostly sound but needs updating.
- Rewrite: useful topic but weak structure or argument.
- Merge: overlaps heavily with another post.
- Retire: outdated, low-value or no longer aligned.
