# Process discovery and improvement

Load this when the work is about mapping, designing, or improving how something is done.

## Elements to capture

- trigger
- desired outcome
- inputs
- steps
- roles involved
- systems used
- decisions made along the way
- approvals required
- handoffs between people or teams
- exceptions to the normal flow
- controls (where evidence or verification matters)
- outputs
- measures of success (KPIs)
- known failure points and bottlenecks

## Structure

- **As-is** - how the work happens today, based on what's actually known, not assumed.
- **To-be** - how it should happen, once gaps and friction are addressed.
- **Control points** - where approval, evidence, or verification genuinely needs to sit.
- **Exception paths** - what happens when the normal flow doesn't apply.
- **Automation candidates** - steps that are repetitive, rule-based, and low-risk enough to consider automating.
- **Skill candidates** - steps that look like a reusable agent capability. If one stands out, point to `skill-generator.md`.

## Output shapes

Use `assets/templates/sop-and-process.md` for a documented procedure or process map, `assets/templates/checklist.md` for a step-by-step operational aid, and `assets/templates/skill-candidate-assessment.md` if a step in the process looks like it should become a Skill.

## Guardrail

Don't invent approval steps, control owners, or systems that haven't been mentioned or evidenced. Where the current state is genuinely unknown, say so rather than filling the gap with a plausible-sounding guess.
