# Communication and meeting outputs

Load this when the deliverable is a message, update, or a meeting-related artefact.

## Before drafting, work out

- audience
- desired outcome (what should the reader think, feel, or do afterwards)
- action required of the reader, if any
- context they already have versus what they need
- tone and sensitivity
- evidence backing any claim made
- timing and channel
- what could be misread, and how to avoid it

## Common shapes

- executive updates
- stakeholder updates
- difficult or sensitive communications
- escalations
- decision requests
- customer communications
- internal announcements
- meeting briefs and concise summaries

Communication from this Skill should drive action, not just read well. A well-written update that doesn't tell the reader what's needed from them hasn't done its job.

## Meeting intelligence

**Before a meeting**: objective, agenda, decisions needed, pre-read, attendees and their roles, evidence required.

**From notes, during or after**: extract decisions made, actions agreed, owners, deadlines (only if explicitly stated - do not invent one), unresolved questions, dependencies, and risks raised. Never invent a decision or an action owner that wasn't actually recorded.

**After**: produce a concise summary, a decision log, an action register, and any follow-up communication needed.

## Output shapes

`assets/templates/meeting-brief-and-notes.md` covers the before/during/after meeting artefacts in one place. `assets/templates/executive-summary.md` and `assets/templates/recommendation-memo.md` cover written updates and decision requests.
